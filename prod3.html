Banana loaf, or banana bread, is a moist, sweet, cake-like bread made from ripe bananas. It's a popular baked good due to its rich flavor, soft texture, and ease of preparation. Typically, the loaf is made with mashed bananas, flour, sugar, eggs, butter or oil, and a leavening agent like baking soda or powder. Some variations include nuts, chocolate chips, or spices like cinnamon for added texture and flavor. Its dense, moist crumb pairs well with tea or coffee and is often enjoyed as a snack or breakfast item.
